Detailed Description

A subclass of Device representing a laser for stimulation.

A subclass of Device representing a laser with stimulation control.

Manages a laser connected to a digital pin, providing control over stimulation timing and trigger modes.

Manages a laser connected to a digital pin, providing methods to control stimulation duration, frequency, and on/off states with cycling behavior.

Manages a laser connected to a digital pin, providing control over stimulation timing and state.
