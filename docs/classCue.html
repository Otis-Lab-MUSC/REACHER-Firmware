Detailed Description

A subclass of Device representing a cue speaker for tone playback.

A subclass of Device representing a cue speaker.

Manages a speaker connected to a digital pin, providing control over tone frequency, duration, and timing.

Manages a speaker connected to a digital pin, providing methods to control tone frequency, duration, and on/off states based on timestamps.
